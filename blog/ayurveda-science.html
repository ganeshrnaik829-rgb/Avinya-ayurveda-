What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is one of the world's oldest holistic healing systems, originating in India more than 3,000 years ago. It emphasizes balance in bodily systems through diet, herbal treatment, and yogic breathing.

Core Principles

  • Doshas: Vata (air & space), Pitta (fire & water), Kapha (earth & water). Each person has a unique combination of these energies.
  • Balance: Health is achieved by balancing the doshas according to individual constitution (Prakriti).
  • Natural Healing: Focuses on prevention and natural treatments using herbs, lifestyle, and nutrition.

Benefits of Ayurveda

  • Helps manage stress and anxiety
  • Improves digestion and metabolism
  • Enhances immunity
  • Promotes balanced lifestyle and well-being
  • Supports natural detoxification

Popular Ayurvedic Remedies

  • Turmeric: Anti-inflammatory and immunity booster
  • Ashwagandha: Reduces stress and improves vitality
  • Triphala: Supports digestion and detoxification
  • Tulsi: Supports respiratory health and stress relief
